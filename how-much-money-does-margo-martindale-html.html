Margo Martindale (born July 18, 1951) is an American actress. In 2011, she won a Primetime Emmy Award and a Critics' Choice Television Award for her recurring role as Mags Bennett on Justified and starting in 2013, she has been nominated for an Emmy Award four times for her recurring role as Claudia on The Americans, winning a second and third Emmy in 2015 and 2016.

Martindale was also nominated for a Tony Award for Best Featured Actress in 2004 for her performance in the play Cat on a Hot Tin Roof. She also voices a character loosely based upon herself in the Netflix animated comedy BoJack Horseman.
