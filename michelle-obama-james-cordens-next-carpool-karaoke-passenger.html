Michelle Obama is James Cordens Next Carpool Karaoke Passenger!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

James Corden’s Carpool Karaoke segment of The Late Late Show has been extremely successful with huge artists such as Sia, Jennifer Hudson, J-Lo and Adele taking to the passenger seat to belt out their biggest hits and covering other people’s songs.

Honestly though, who can forget Adele’s infamous rapping of Nicki Minaj’s verse from Kanye West’s Monster!?

But now, Carpool Karaoke is stepping up a notch as it has been confirmed that the First Lady Michelle Obama is up next to sing with James!

Michelle recently made a new snapchat account for her upcoming trips to Spain, Morocco and Liberia.

She tweeted about her new account with a photo of herself with the sassy hand emoji!
