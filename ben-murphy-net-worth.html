What is Ben Murphy's Net Worth?

Ben Murphy is an American actor who has a net worth of $4 million. Ben Murphy was born in Jonesboro, Arkansas in March 1942. From 1968 to 1970 he starred as Joe Sample on the television series The Name of the Game. Murphy starred as Jed "Kid" Curry on the TV series Alias Smith and Jones from 1971 to 1973. From 1973 to 1974 he starred as Mike Murdoch on the series Griff and in 1976 he starred as Sam Casey on the TV mini-series Gemini Man.

Ben Murphy starred as Will Chisholm on the television series The Chisholms from 1979 to 1980 and starred as Patrick Sean Flaherty on the TV series Lottery! From 1983 to 1984. Murphy starred as Paul Berrenger on the series Berrenger's in 1985 and as Lieutenant Danko on the series The Dirty Dozen in 1988. He has starred in several films including Yours, Mine, and Ours, Heatwave!, and The Genesis Code.
