Who is Pabllo Vittar?

Pabllo Vittar is a Brazilian singer, songwriter, and drag queen. Born Phabullo Rodrigues da Silva on November 1, 1993, Vittar gained fame with his debut single Open Bar in 2015. He is renowned for his vibrant pop music and activism for LGBTQ+ rights.

What are his most popular songs?

Some of his most popular songs include K.O., Corpo Sensual, Sua Cara with Major Lazer and Anitta, and Parabéns. His music often features a blend of pop, electronic, and Brazilian genres.

What awards has Pabllo Vittar won?

He has won numerous awards, including the MTV Europe Music Award for Best Brazilian Act. He also became the first drag queen to be nominated for a Latin Grammy Award.
