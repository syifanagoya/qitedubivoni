Paul George Bio, Age, Family, Siblings, And Childhood

Who is Paul George? Paul George was born on May 2, 1990, in Palmdale, California, United States of America. Paul George Sr. is his dad, and Paulette Ann George is his girlfriend. Teiosha and Portala are his two older sisters. Their parents fostered an atmosphere where they were allowed to participate in sports from a young age. Teiosha attended Pepperdine University in Malibu, California, and Portala attended California State University, San Bernardino.

Paul grew up idolizing Kobe Bryant and rooting for the Los Angeles Lakers and Los Angeles Clippers. Though he has been playing basketball since he was a teenager, it was mainly his sister Teiosha who taught him the basics. Paul is 33 years old and was born under the sign of Taurus. Professional Life, Career & Living Style

Paul George declared for the NBA draft in 2010. He was drafted as the 10th overall selection by the Indiana Pacers. On July 1, he officially joined the Pacers after agreeing to a two-year, $3.9 million deferred rookie deal. His breakthrough season identified him as one of the league’s best stars. On August 1, 2014, he suffered a compound fracture of both bones in his lower right leg.

A pin was implanted into his leg during surgery. He appeared in 81 games during the regular season, averaging 23.1 points per game. The same year, he was named to the NBA All-Star game.

Girlfriend, Marriage & Relationship Status

Who is Paul George’s Girlfriend? In the past, Paul George dated Callie Rivers, the daughter of Los Angeles Clippers head coach Doc Rivers. They ended their relationship in 2013 when it was announced that he had impregnated an exotic dancer called Daniela Rajic. At the time, there was a lot of talk about George giving Rajic $1 million to end the pregnancy. However, his spokesman informed the media in 2014 that the allegations were unfounded. Rajic lodged a paternity suit against him a couple of months back. It was eventually determined that George was the parent.
