Where to Find Warm Safflina Tears of the Kingdom? Tears of the Kingdom Warm Safflina Location

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, February 17, 2024
Where to Find Warm Safflina Tears of the Kingdom? Gerudo Canyon and Desert are the best locations to find the Warm Safflina in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom.

Where to Find Warm Safflina Tears Of The Kingdom?

The Warm Safflina flower possesses remarkable utility, providing notable benefits. When used in dishes, it grants a Cold Resistance effect for a duration of 2 minutes and 30 seconds per piece consumed. Moreover, as you progress and seek to augment your armor through the aid of the Great Fairies, this flower becomes an essential ingredient for upgrading your Snowquill Armor, enabling you to navigate the icy terrains of Hyrule with greater ease.

While the Warm Safflina can be stumbled upon in various locations throughout the game's expansive map, there are two specific areas where you can actively search for it. Firstly, it can be found on the northwest slopes of Death Mountain. To begin your search in this region, embark from the Mayak Shrine and ascend the mountain through the East Deplian Badlands.

Along this slope, you will discover a plentiful supply of Warm Safflina. A significant advantage of gathering it here is the absence of weather-related concerns that would otherwise be encountered in the Gerudo Desert. However, if you are determined to venture into the scorching heat of the Gerudo Desert in search of this precious flower, there are a few precautions to consider.

First and foremost, ensure you wear suitable attire capable of withstanding the intense heat. Once properly equipped, make your way to the Karahatag Shrine and descend to the location where the Shrine is situated amidst the sand. Here, you will encounter a substantial quantity of Warm Safflina. It is worth noting that while the flower may also be found in other parts of the desert, this particular area boasts the highest concentration and guarantees a successful discovery.

Tears Of The Kingdom Warm Safflina Location

Warm Safflina is spread across the vast expanse of Gerudo, specifically exploring Gerudo Canyon and the Desert region. As this particular variant of Safflina thrives in scorching climates, Gerudo becomes an ideal destination for its discovery. Abundant locations are scattered throughout with this botanical gem at coordinates (-3093, -2999, 0013).

Alternatively, for those who may find Gerudo Desert's climatic conditions somewhat daunting, a journey northward to Eldin presents another viable option. Exploring the vicinity of Death Mountain unveils additional spawn sites for the Warm Safflina. Notably, one can find a specimen near the Foothill Stable, precisely at coordinates (2598, 1200, 0148). Furthermore, while traversing the mountainous terrain in a northerly direction, one can discover several more locales, including a handful in the elevated Deplian Badlands.

Zelda Tears Of The Kingdom Warm Safflina

The Warm Safflina material is a versatile ingredient that can be used for various purposes. It is commonly used for enhancing armor, providing cold resistance, and cooking. This medicinal plant specifically grows in hot regions like the Gerudo Desert, and it has a warm sensation when touched. When cooked in a dish, it increases your cold resistance.

One of the recipes you can create using Warm Safflina is Steamed Fruit, but there are many other recipes that can benefit from its properties. Additionally, the Warm Safflina Plant can be utilized to upgrade specific armor pieces such as the Snowquill Headdress, Snowquill Trousers, and Snowquill Tunic, enhancing their effectiveness.

To acquire Warm Safflina, it is recommended to explore the Gerudo region, especially Gerudo Canyon and the Desert. Since this particular variant of Safflina spawns in hot climates, Gerudo is an excellent location for finding it. There are numerous spots within the area where you can spot this flower, and the coordinates (-3093, -2999, 0013) indicate one such location.
