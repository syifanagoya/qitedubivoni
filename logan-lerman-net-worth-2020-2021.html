Early Life & Biography

Logan Lerman, whose full name is Logan Wade Lerman was born on the 19th of January 1992 in Beverly Hills, California, the U.S. to parents, Lisa Lerman (formerly, Goldman), and Larry Lerman.  His mother works as his manager while his father is a businessman and orthotist. He has an elder sister, Lindsey Lerman, and an elder brother Lucas Lerman.

He belongs to a family of medical professionals, and his family owns and operates an orthotics and prosthetics company. When it comes to his education, he had attended Beverly Hills High School, and he got accepted in 2010 to study creative writing at New York University, but he postponed his attendance. Coming to his religion, he is Jewish, and his grandparents were born in 4 different countries, namely Germany, Mexico, Poland, and the U.S.

Personal Life

Career

Logan Lerman began acting at an early age itself. As a child actor, he has worked in movies such as “The Patriot” (2000), “What Women Want” (2000), and “Riding in Cars with Boys” (2001). His performance as a child actor in the 2003 TV movie, “A Painted House,” was very well received, even landing him the Young Artist Award for Best Performance by a Leading Young Actor in television production.

His performance in the 2004 TV series “Jack & Bobby” as a 12-year-old extremely bright social misfit brought him another Young Artist Award win. His first starring role in a motion picture came with the 2006 comedy “Hoot,” where his performance brought him the Young Artist Award for the third time. He then appeared in movies “The Number 23” (2007), “3:10 to Yuma” (2007), and “Meet Bill” (2008).

In 2010, he played the lead role of Percy Jackson in the American fantasy adventure movie “Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief,” which brought him much well deserved world-wide recognition.  He starred in the 2012 coming-of-age drama film “The Perks of Being a Wallflower,” where he once played the lead role. The movie received a great response, just like Logan’s performance, which landed him a Teen Choice Award win for Choice Movie Actor: Drama.

In the years that followed, he starred in movies “Stuck in Love” (2012) and “Percy Jackson: Sea of Monsters” (2013). He then starred in the 2014 Biblical epic “Noah,” where his performance was critically well-received. His most recent work was in the 2020 Amazon web series “Hunters,” where he is in the lead role. Logan is a close friend of popular Actor Dean Collins with whom he has collaborated on comedic short films.

They jointly write, direct and shoot these videos with some occasional help from family and friends. These videos are uploaded to YouTube under the joint account name of “monkeynuts1069”. Logan formed the band, Indigo with Dean Collins and musician Daniel Pashman in 2006. In the band, Logan plays keyboard and guitar; Daniel plays the drums while Collins sings the lead vocals.

Awards & Achievements

Logan Lerman has earned numerous Awards and Achievements over the years. Here is a list covering some of those:

  • He has won the Young Artist Award 3 times for his performance in the 2006 movie “Hoot,” in the 2003 TV movie “A Painted House,” and in the 2004-2005 TV series “Jack & Bobby.”
  • He was the winner of the Chopard Trophy in the category of Male Revelation at the 2014 Cannes Film Festival.
  • He was the winner of the Virtuoso Award at the 2015 Santa Barbara International Film Festival for his role in the movie “Fury” (2014).
  • He was the winner of the Choice Movie Actor: Drama award for his performance in the movie “The Perks of Being a Wallflower” at the 2013 Teen Choice Awards.
