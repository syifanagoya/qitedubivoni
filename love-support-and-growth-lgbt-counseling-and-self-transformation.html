Navigating Identity Within the LGBT Spectrum: Self-Discovery and Growth

Navigating your identity within the LBGT spectrum is extremely challenging, especially as society’s expectations and pressures are much heavier on the members of the LGBT community. Online LGBTQ+ therapy on Calmerry creates a safe space and guides you through the many challenges of identity and acceptance.

Love, Support, and Growth with LGBT Counseling

The members of the LGBT community suffer from mental health issues at a much higher rate. Online therapy makes mental health support accessible, affordable, and more convenient, and you can join every session from the comfort and safety of your own home.

LGBTQIA2S+ counseling is a specialized form of psychotherapy, and this may not be available in every area. Each specialized therapist has experience in addressing the unique mental health issues of people who may identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and many other non-heteronormative sexual orientations and gender identities.

LGBT counseling offers customizable sessions with licensed therapists specialized in coming out, identity, family conflicts, relationships, discrimination, and related mental health issues, such as stress and depression.

Licensed therapists in this field differ from other types of mental health therapists. They use queer affirmative therapy – an approach that accepts and affirms any sexual orientation and gender identity. Online counseling goes beyond tolerance – it actively validates and celebrates your unique experience and helps you manage challenges, learn how to live authentically and eliminate internalized stigmas.

Types of LBGT Mental Health Therapy: Love, Support, and Self-Growth

Generally, there are two types of mental health therapy for members of the LGBT community:

  • Affirmative therapy, which affirms, validates, and celebrates each identity and sexual orientation.
  • Gender therapy, which targets issues related to gender identity – such as exploring your gender and transitioning, gender dysphoria, coming out as non-binary or transgender, and provides guidance on matters such as surgical options and hormonal treatments, and offers coping mechanisms on psychological and emotional aspects.

LGBT therapy is not widely accessible, even though many online platforms now offer online counseling. Calmerry is one of the few platforms that provides licensed therapists experienced in affirmative therapy and gender therapy.

Is LGBT Counseling Suitable for Severe Conditions?

LGBT counseling helps to address many issues and concerns – ranging from self-care and self-love to dealing with internalized social stigma, family issues, trauma and abuse, discrimination, and many more.

Online therapy is not suitable for urgent life-threatening conditions, such as suicidal thoughts or psychosis. In these cases, urgent medical attention is required.

However, LGBT online counseling is suitable for individuals dealing with severe conditions, as long as they receive other forms of treatment as well, such as in-person sessions and medical treatment.
