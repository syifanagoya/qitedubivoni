Biography

Hank Johnson was born in Washington, DC on October 2, 1954. Democrat who became the U.S. Representative for Georgia’s 4th congressional district in 2007. He has served under the Committee on Armed Services and the Committee on the Judiciary. He was the first Democratic congressman in Georgia to publicly endorse Barack Obama in the 2008 Democratic presidential race. He graduated from Texas Southern University’s Thurgood Marshall School of Law in Houston in 1979 before practicing law in Decatur, Georgia, for more than 25 years.
